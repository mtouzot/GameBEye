GameBEye.gbcamcolors package
============================

The **GameBEye.gbcamcolors** package is as its name indicates focused on colors and color space conversion. It deals with a selection of color palettes (picked and adapted from `HerrZatacke <https://github.com/HerrZatacke>`_ `gb-printer-web <https://github.com/HerrZatacke/gb-printer-web>`_).
