Colors module

The gamebeye.gbcamcolors and gamebeye.gbcampalettes enumerations are as its name indicates focused on colors and color palettes. It deals with a selection of color palettes (picked and adapted from HerrZatacke gb-printer-web).

The module gamebeye.color_helpers module gather color conversions.
